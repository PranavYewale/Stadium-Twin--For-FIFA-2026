🚀 Thrilled to share my submission for Prompt War Challenge 4: Arena.Twin OS—a next-generation Stadium Digital Twin AI Operating System built for the FIFA World Cup 2026! 🏟️✨

Managing stadium-wide logistics for 80,000+ spectators isn't just about security—it's about real-time coordination, crowd psychology, and utility optimization. Arena.Twin OS acts as a unified digital control center, connecting simulated IoT sensors, camera computer vision streams, and ticketing gates to a premium operator dashboard.

Here is what makes Arena.Twin OS stand out:

🔮 3D Digital Twin (WebGL/Three.js): Interactive, color-coded seating stands (Green/Yellow/Red) showing live spectator density at a glance.
🗺️ Live GIS perimeter Map (Leaflet.js): Geospatial mapping of entry gates, parking occupancies, transit arrivals, and flashing volunteer dispatch points.
⚡ Real-Time WebSockets Engine: Driven by Flask-SocketIO to update telemetry vectors and power grids every 3 seconds.
🧰 Four Specialist Operations Assistants:
  1️⃣ Lost Child Radar: Automatically runs camera vision checks, predicts movement direction based on crowd flow, alerts nearby volunteers, and flashes radar beacons on the map!
  2️⃣ Accessible transit Router: Compiles step-free routes, forecasts elevator wait times, and provides audio guides for visually impaired fans.
  3️⃣ AI Queue Optimizer: Dynamically balances concession stand checkouts, shifting resources from clear lanes to congested courts to lower wait times.
  4️⃣ Fan Assistance Portal: A state-aware NLP chatbot answering transit schedules, restroom locations, and food stock queries.
🧠 Google Gemini AI Decision Brain: Parses active telemetry/alarms to suggest priority dispatches and generate multilingual public announcement warnings (with native Read Aloud audio).
🔋 Smart eco-Grid Monitor: Tracks battery banks, HVAC loads, and water flow rates to scale carbon footprint scores in real-time.
🚨 Scenario Command: Overrides to trigger simulated evacuations, grid power outages, heatwaves, or flash crowds to test and train safety procedures.

🎨 Built with a sleek slate-carbon glassmorphic dashboard theme, flat-tech bordering, responsive mobile layouts, and custom bottom-right slide-in notification toasts.
